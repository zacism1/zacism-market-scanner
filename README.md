# zacism-market-scanner

Public Investment Mode research and market scanner results from the Zacism trading app.

Published manually from the Zacism iOS/macOS app into the `data/` directory.

The homepage derives **Weekly Top 5 Investment Ideas** from published Investment Mode reports and qualifying Global Scan `BUY` ideas. It deduplicates tickers, requires at least 70/100 conviction, and prioritises different published industries before filling any remaining places.

Investment Mode also publishes a searchable universe of **500 global long-duration themes** across 15 categories, with region and exchange metadata. Grok's weekly ranked themes sit on top of that deterministic catalog; the catalog does not disappear when a model refresh fails. Theme cards are discovery prompts, while ticker validation and ranking happen in Zacism's web-grounded industry funnel.

**Published research data is for informational purposes only. Not financial advice.**
